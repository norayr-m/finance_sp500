super-exponential regime study · S&P 500 · 7041.28 · 2026-04-17

A year ago — April 2025 — I sketched an S&P 500 model and pushed it to GitHub predicting the index would hit 7000 in spring 2026.

On April 17, 2026 it closed at 7041.28.

This repo is the cleaned-up analysis behind that call. The framework I derived by eye (straight line through two local minima, then an ln(ln(price)) linearity test) turns out to match Didier Sornette's Log-Periodic Power Law Singularity model, which has been publishing this thesis since 1996. I just didn't know.
